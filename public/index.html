Code

I work at Square as an engineering manager on the platform engineering team.

I am very active in the open source community, sourcing over 50 projects on Github including the Enki blog platform, TufteGraph for javascript graphing and Kronic for date formatting and parsing. I am a core team member of RSpec and Bundler, and a Ruby on Rails contributor.

I have spoken at RubyConf, RailsConf, and many regional conferences. You can find recordings at Confreaks.

In 2010 I travelled the world running a series of Ruby on Rails workshops titled Your Database Is Your Friend Training. I blog about code at Robot Has No Heart and used to write a weekly column for RubySource.

I have worked on many big commercial Ruby on Rails projects including Square (current), The Conversation, ClearGrain, and Redbubble. I made and maintain the Vegan Melbourne website.
