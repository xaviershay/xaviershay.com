Xavier Shay

Hello, pleased to be meeting you. I make computers do wonderful things, swing dance, enjoy exercise, live vegan, and I teach others how to do the same. I am in my mid-twenties and live in Melbourne, Australia.

I reply to all email sent to hello@xaviershay.com, so do write.

Code

I am very active in the open source community: I am a DataMapper commiter, have contributed to Ruby on Rails, and I wrote the Enki blog platform, as well as many other widely used libraries such as TufteGraph for javascript graphing and Kronic for date formatting and parsing.

In 2010 I travelled the world running a series of Ruby on Rails workshops titled Your Database Is Your Friend Training. I regularly organise and speak at the Melbourne Ruby on Rails Meetup, and I blog about code at Robot Has No Heart.

Personal Development

My brother and I write the personal development blog TwoShay, as well as running a regularly month long vegan mentoring group Vegan Month.

Swing Dancing

I have been swing dancing for more than five years. I irregularly teach around Melbourne, and run two dancing related websites: LindyLog for keeping a dance diary, and EventHand for dance event management. I have also written an iPhone app Swing Bingo.

The Rest

I use Twitter, Goodreads and Dailymile actively. Facebook reluctantly.
